## Description

BIM VisitPlan is a mobile workspace for BIM Group teams to plan, manage, and review client visits from the field.

The app brings daily schedules, client records, visit outcomes, and team reporting into one secure workflow. Users sign in with their Microsoft work account, see the visits and clients available to their role, and keep activity records up to date while working with customers.

Key features:

- Secure Microsoft Entra ID sign-in
- Today dashboard with visit status, completion, and pending outcome indicators
- Weekly visit planning with client, date, time, department, participant, and agenda details
- Client directory with search, sector filters, account type, status, and visit counts
- Client workspace for profile details, contacts, instructions, visit history, and quick scheduling
- Visit outcome updates for status, result, notes, reschedule details, and pipeline value
- Individual reports for recent visits, completion rate, outcomes, and pipeline activity
- Team reporting for management, including AM, department, and sector drilldowns
- Admin tools for user approvals, client management, sector setup, and role-based access

BIM VisitPlan is designed for sales, solution, account management, management, and admin users who need a clear view of customer engagement activity across departments and sectors.

Access requires an approved BIM Group work account.
